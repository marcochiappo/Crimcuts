About CrimCutz

CrimCutz was created by three Harvard boys — Marco, Aaryan, and Matthew — who were tired of watching their friends get absolutely violated by bad trims.

After years of seeing your boy get wronged at La Flamme Barber Shop, or getting unlucky at Atlas with the wrong boss man, we knew something had to change.

Our Mission

  • Marco: “No more accidental bowl cuts. No more trust falls with barbers you've never met.”
  • Aaryan: “We’re making sure your fade is cleaner than your GPA.”
  • Matthew: “Harvard men deserve trims worthy of their tuition.”

CrimCutz is the solution: a simple, student-friendly platform to help your bros get a decent trim — every single time.
